Mission Statement

Kiddie Korner Ratings is a site designed to help parents identify movies that are appropriate for there family via the Motion Picture Association of America ratings system. It is used to rate a film's suitability for certain audiences based on its content. There are 5 ratings: G, PG, PG-13, R, NC-17. At Kiddie Korner we focus on movies that are G which are suitable for all ages, PG which requires Parental Guidance for material that might not be suitable for children, and the PG-13 rating is for material that may be inappropriate for children under 13, but we recommend parents do what is best for their child & household.
